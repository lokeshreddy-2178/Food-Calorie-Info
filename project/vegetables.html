Vegetables and Their Calorie Information

Vegetables are a cornerstone of a healthy diet, providing essential nutrients while being very low in calories. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants, making them great for overall health and weight management. Leafy greens like spinach and kale are especially low in calories, with just around 7 calories per cup of raw spinach. Cruciferous vegetables like broccoli and cauliflower are also low-calorie options, with a cup of broccoli containing about 55 calories. Because of their high water and fiber content, vegetables help you feel full and satisfied without adding many calories to your diet, making them ideal for anyone aiming to maintain or lose weight while nourishing their body.
