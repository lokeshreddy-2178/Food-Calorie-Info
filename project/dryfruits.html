Dry Fruits

Dry fruits are nutrient-dense snacks that are rich in vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats. They can support heart health, improve digestion, and provide energy.Dry fruits, or dried fruits, are nutrient-dense snacks created by removing moisture from fresh fruits, which intensifies their sweetness and concentrates their nutritional value. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making them beneficial for heart health and digestion. Popular varieties like apricots, figs, and raisins offer unique flavors and health benefits. Incorporating dry fruits into your diet provides a convenient, healthy option for snacking or adding to meals.
