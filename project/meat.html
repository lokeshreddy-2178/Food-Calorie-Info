Meats and Their Calorie Information

Meat is an excellent source of protein and essential nutrients. Different types of meat vary in calorie content and nutritional value. Lean meats like chicken and turkey provide high protein with lower fat content, while red meats such as beef and lamb are higher in calories. Incorporating a variety of meats into your diet can help maintain a balanced intake of nutrients.
