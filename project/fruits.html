Fruits and Their Calorie Information

Fruits are a nutritious and essential part of a balanced diet, offering a wide range of vitamins, minerals, and fiber, all while being relatively low in calories. Many fruits, like apples, berries, and oranges, provide natural sugars that give the body a quick source of energy without the high calorie count found in processed snacks. For example, an apple contains around 95 calories, while a cup of strawberries has just about 50 calories. Bananas, being slightly higher in calories at around 105 per medium fruit, also offer more substantial energy and are rich in potassium. Overall, fruits are a great way to satisfy sweet cravings and maintain a healthy calorie intake.
